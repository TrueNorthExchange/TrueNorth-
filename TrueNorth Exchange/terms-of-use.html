An order is considered paid once:
• the deposit has received the required number of confirmations on the network; and
• an additional period (up to 4 minutes) has passed to allow full crediting of funds to the service’s or liquidity provider’s balance.
8.1.2. Increased Number of Confirmations
For TRC20, ERC20, TON, BTC networks, and, in exceptional cases, XMR, funds may initially be credited to the service’s internal node before being forwarded to the exchange.
